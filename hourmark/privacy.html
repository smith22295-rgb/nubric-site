Hour Mark

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

Hour Mark is built for 1099 contractors and the businesses that pay them. We take privacy seriously — your data stays yours. We do not sell your data, ever.

1. Who We Are

Hour Mark is a mobile application developed and published by Nubric LLC, a limited liability company based in Phoenix, Arizona. You can reach us at hello@nubric.dev.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide the app's core functionality.

If you sign in with Google or Apple:

  • Your name, email address, and profile photo provided by Google or Apple
  • A unique user ID assigned by Firebase Authentication

Data you enter into the app:

  • Your business name, address, and Employer Identification Number (EIN) or Social Security Number (SSN)
  • Contractor names, addresses, and Taxpayer Identification Numbers (TINs / SSNs)
  • Hourly rates, hours worked, and payment amounts
  • Job names and notes

If you use Local Mode (no account):

  • All data is stored only on your device using local storage. Nothing is transmitted to our servers.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to provide the features of Hour Mark:

  • Displaying your logged hours and payment history
  • Generating 1099-NEC summary reports
  • Syncing your data across your devices (signed-in accounts only)
  • Identifying you when you sign back in

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the app's core function.

4. How We Store Your Data

Cloud (signed-in accounts): Your data is stored in Google Firebase Firestore, a secure cloud database. Firebase uses industry-standard encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest. Firebase is operated by Google LLC. You can review Google's data practices at firebase.google.com/support/privacy.

Local Mode: Data is stored exclusively on your device using AsyncStorage. Uninstalling the app will permanently delete this data.

5. Sensitive Financial and Tax Information

Hour Mark allows you to store sensitive information such as Social Security Numbers (SSNs), Employer Identification Numbers (EINs), and payment amounts. This data is used solely for generating tax-related summaries within the app. We recommend using the app on a personal, secured device with a screen lock enabled.

TINs and SSNs are masked by default within the app's interface and are only revealed when you choose to show them.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • Firebase / Google: For authentication and cloud storage as described above
  • Legal requirements: If required by law, court order, or governmental authority

7. Data Retention and Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from within the app (Setup → Delete Account). This permanently removes all your data from our servers within 30 days. Data stored in Local Mode can be deleted by uninstalling the app.

If you contact us directly at hello@nubric.dev, we will assist with any data deletion request.

8. Children's Privacy

Hour Mark is intended for use by adults managing business or contractor payment records. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under the age of 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided personal information, we will delete it promptly.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export a copy of your data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@nubric.dev.

10. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data. All data transmitted between the app and Firebase is encrypted using TLS. We recommend protecting your device with a passcode or biometric lock and using a strong, unique password for your Google or Apple account.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of material changes by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us:

  • Email: hello@nubric.dev
  • Website: nubric.dev
  • Nubric LLC, Phoenix, Arizona
