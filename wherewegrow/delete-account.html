WhereWeGrow

Delete Your Account

Effective: April 19, 2026  ·  Last updated: April 19, 2026

WhereWeGrow is developed by Nubric LLC. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time — directly in the app, or by emailing us if you no longer have access.

1. Delete in the App (Recommended)

  1. Open WhereWeGrow on your phone
  2. Tap the Profile tab
  3. Scroll to the bottom of the Profile screen
  4. Tap Delete Account and confirm

Deletion happens immediately. You will be signed out and all your data will be permanently removed.

2. What Gets Deleted

  • Your Firebase authentication account (Google or Apple sign-in link)
  • Your profile information (name, email)
  • Saved favorites and bookmarked crops
  • Location history and search history
  • Any feedback you submitted through the app (tied to your account)
  • All settings and preferences
  • All locally cached data on the device

3. What Is Retained

Nothing. Deletion is immediate and permanent. We do not retain backups or shadow copies of your account data after deletion.

Anonymous, aggregated analytics (which screens were viewed, which features were used, which crops were tapped) may have already been recorded, but this data contains no personal information and cannot be traced back to you or your account after deletion.

4. Guest Users

If you use WhereWeGrow as a guest (without signing in), there is no account to delete on our servers. To remove any local data stored on your device, simply uninstall the app. Location permissions can be revoked at any time through your device's system settings.

5. Can't Access the App?

If you no longer have the app installed or cannot sign in, email us and we will delete your account on your behalf:

  • Email: info@nubric.dev
  • Subject line: Account Deletion Request
  • Please include the email address associated with your WhereWeGrow account

We will process the request within 30 days and confirm by replying to your email once deletion is complete.
