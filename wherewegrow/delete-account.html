Where We Grow

Clearing Your Data

Effective: May 1, 2026

Where We Grow does not require an account. There is no sign-in, no profile, and no personal identifier collected — so there is no account to delete. All of your favorites and saved data live only on your device, and you can clear them at any time.

1. Clear Your Data in the App

  1. Open Where We Grow on your phone
  2. Tap the About tab
  3. Tap Clear All Data and confirm

This removes your favorites, viewed-crop counters, cached crop and market data, and any other data the App has stored on your device.

2. Uninstall the App

Uninstalling Where We Grow from your device also removes all local data the App has stored. You can then revoke location permissions through your device's system settings.

3. What's Stored on Our Servers

Because Where We Grow has no accounts, there is no personal data tied to you on our servers. The only data we collect is:

  • Anonymous usage analytics — random device-generated identifier, screen views, feature taps. Cannot be traced back to you.
  • Anonymous feedback — only the message text and star rating you chose to submit, with no name or email.

See the Privacy Policy for full details.

4. Requesting Removal of Anonymous Feedback

If you submitted feedback and would like it removed, email us with the approximate date of submission and a portion of the message text so we can locate it. Because feedback is submitted anonymously, we cannot verify ownership — but we will remove any feedback you can identify in good faith.

  • Email: info@nubric.dev
  • Subject line: Where We Grow Data Request

We will respond within 30 days.
