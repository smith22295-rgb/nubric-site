WhereWeGrow

Privacy Policy

Effective: April 2, 2026

WhereWeGrow is built for curious minds who want to understand the land around them. We believe in transparency — your location data is used to show you crops, not to track you. We do not sell your data, ever.

1. Who We Are

WhereWeGrow is a mobile application developed and published by Nubric LLC ("Nubric," "we," "us," or "our"), a limited liability company based in Arizona, United States. You can reach us at info@nubric.dev.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

When you create an account using Google Sign-In or Apple Sign-In, we receive your name and email address from the authentication provider. We do not receive or store your password.

Automatically Collected Information

  • Location Data: With your permission, we collect your device's approximate location to show you crops growing near you. Location data is used in real-time and may be stored in your account history if you are signed in. You can deny location access and the App will use a default location instead.
  • Usage Data: We may collect information about which crops you view, your favorite crops, and your interaction with the App to improve your experience.
  • Device Information: We may collect device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers for analytics and crash reporting.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the App
  • Show you crop data relevant to your location
  • Save your favorites and location history to your account
  • Improve and personalize your experience
  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • Monitor usage patterns and fix bugs

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase: Authentication, data storage (Firestore), and analytics. Google's privacy policy applies to data processed by Firebase. See Firebase Privacy.
  • USDA CropScape API: We send location coordinates to the USDA CropScape Cropland Data Layer service to retrieve satellite-derived crop data. No personal information is sent to USDA.
  • USDA Local Food Portal API: We query the USDA Local Food Portal to show nearby farmers markets and on-farm markets. Only location coordinates are sent; no personal information is shared.
  • TheMealDB API: We query this public recipe database to show crop-related recipes. No personal information is shared.
  • Apple Sign-In / Google Sign-In: Authentication is handled by Apple and Google respectively. We receive only your name and email.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your account data is stored in Google Firebase Firestore, which uses industry-standard encryption in transit and at rest. We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at info@nubric.dev.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only in the following cases:

  • With your consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data and account
  • Withdraw consent for location tracking at any time through your device settings
  • Use the App as a guest without creating an account

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@nubric.dev.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy within the App and updating the effective date. Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
