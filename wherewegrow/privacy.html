Where We Grow

Privacy Policy

Effective: May 6, 2026

Nubric LLC ("Nubric," "we," "us," or "our") operates the Where We Grow mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our App.

The short version: Where We Grow does not require an account. We do not collect your name, email, or any personal identifier. Your favorites and saved data live only on your device.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Where We Grow has no sign-in, no user accounts, and no profile system. We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other personal contact information.

2. Information We Do Collect

Location Data. With your permission, we use your device's approximate location to show you crops growing near you. Location data is processed in real-time and sent to the USDA CropScape service to retrieve crop data for your area. We do not store a history of your locations on any server. You can deny location access and the App remains fully usable — you can search by city instead.

Anonymous Usage Analytics. We collect anonymous, aggregated usage events such as which screens you view, which crops you tap, and which map layers you toggle. This data is associated with a random anonymous identifier generated on your device the first time the App runs — it is not tied to your name, email, Apple ID, Google account, or any other identity.

Feedback You Choose to Submit. If you submit feedback through the App's "Send Feedback" form, we store the message text and star rating you provided, along with a timestamp. We do not collect your name or email when you submit feedback.

On-Device Storage. Your favorite crops, viewed-crop counters, and cached crop and market data are stored locally on your device. They never leave your device. You can clear all of this from the About tab inside the App.

3. Tracking Disclosure

We do not track you across other companies' apps or websites. We do not use Apple's advertising identifier (IDFA) or any cross-app tracking technologies. Our analytics are anonymous, aggregated, and used solely to improve the App.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Google Firebase: Server-side API proxies and anonymous feedback storage. Firebase Analytics collects the anonymous usage data described above. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • USDA CropScape API: We send location coordinates to the USDA CropScape Cropland Data Layer service to retrieve satellite-derived crop data. No personal information is sent.
  • USDA Local Food Portal API: We query the Local Food Portal to show nearby farmers markets, on-farm markets, CSA programs, and U-Pick farms. Only location coordinates are sent.
  • California Department of Water Resources Statewide Crop Mapping (Land IQ) (via our server): For users in California, we query the CA DWR field-polygon dataset to identify crops with higher accuracy than satellite imagery alone provides. Only location coordinates are sent; no personal information is shared.
  • OpenStreetMap Nominatim (via our server): When you search for a city, our server-side proxy queries Nominatim. No personal information is shared.
  • TheMealDB API: We query this public recipe database to show crop-related recipes. No personal information is shared.

5. Data Storage and Security

Anonymous feedback submissions are stored in Google Firebase Firestore using industry-standard encryption in transit and at rest. Anonymous analytics events are stored in Google Analytics. All other data — including your favorites, viewed-crop counters, and cached map data — is stored only on your device and never transmitted.

6. Data Processing Location

The limited anonymous data we do collect is processed on Google servers in the United States. By using the App, you consent to this processing.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent any information to third parties. We may share information only with your explicit consent, to comply with legal obligations, or to protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property.

8. Your Rights

Because Where We Grow does not require an account and does not collect personal identifiers, there is nothing on our servers tied to you specifically. You can:

  • Withdraw consent for location access at any time through your device's Settings
  • Clear all data the App has stored on your device, from the About tab
  • Request information about the App's data handling by contacting us

9. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. The App contains no social features and no user-to-user communication.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you by posting the new policy and updating the effective date.
