MotoTrax

Delete Your Account

Effective: May 3, 2026  ·  Last updated: May 3, 2026

MotoTrax is built by Nubric LLC. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time — directly in the app, or by emailing us if you no longer have access.

1. Delete in the App (Recommended)

  1. Open MotoTrax on your phone or the web
  2. Tap the menu icon (hamburger) in the top-left
  3. Select Settings
  4. Tap Delete Account and confirm

Deletion happens immediately. You will be signed out and your account will be queued for permanent removal. We confirm deletion by email within 7 days.

2. What Gets Deleted

  • Your profile (display name, username, riding number, home state, riding level, race classes, bikes)
  • Your authentication account (Apple, Google, or email/password sign-in link)
  • All posts, comments, reviews, ratings, and condition reports you submitted
  • All session check-ins and ride log entries
  • All follows, RSVPs, and reactions
  • Track ownership claims you submitted (verification is revoked)
  • Any documents you uploaded to support an ownership claim

3. What Is Retained

Active database records are removed within 30 days of your deletion request. Encrypted backups are purged within 90 days. After that, nothing about your account remains.

Anonymous, aggregated analytics (which screens were viewed, which features were used) may have already been recorded, but this data contains no personal information and cannot be traced back to you or your account after deletion.

4. Track Ownership Claims

If you were a verified track owner, deleting your account revokes your verification and removes your ownership posts. The track itself remains in the directory (it's a public listing) but is no longer attributed to you. The track can be re-claimed by another verified owner.

5. Can't Access the App?

If you no longer have the app installed or cannot sign in, email us and we will delete your account on your behalf:

  • Email: info@nubric.dev
  • Subject line: Account Deletion Request
  • Please include the email address associated with your MotoTrax account

We will process the request within 30 days and confirm by replying to your email once deletion is complete.
