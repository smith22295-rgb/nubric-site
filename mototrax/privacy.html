MotoTrax

Privacy Policy

Effective: May 3, 2026  ·  Last updated: May 3, 2026

MotoTrax is built by Nubric. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and the controls you have — in plain English. We do not sell your data.

1. Who This Policy Applies To

Anyone who uses the MotoTrax app on iOS, Android, or the web (mototrax.app). You must be at least 13 years old to create a MotoTrax account. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

2. What We Collect

Account information you provide

  • Display name, username, email address
  • Riding number, home state, riding level, race classes, bikes you ride
  • Track ownership claim documents you upload (LLC papers, OHV permits, AMA charter certificates, etc.)

Authentication identifiers

  • If you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive the verified email address and a stable identifier from the provider. We do not see or store your password.
  • If you sign in with email + password, the password is hashed and stored by our authentication provider (Firebase Auth). We never see the plaintext.

Content you create

Posts, comments, reviews, ratings, condition reports, session check-ins, follows, RSVPs, reactions, and reports you submit on tracks.

Location data

  • When you tap "Use My Location" on the Map, your device shares your current GPS coordinates with the MotoTrax app only. The coordinates are used to center the map and (in the future) to show tracks near you. We do not transmit your location to MotoTrax servers and we do not retain it after you close the app, unless you explicitly choose to share it (for example, by checking in to a track).
  • Track check-ins you create do save the track's coordinates (not your live location) to your account.

Device + app data

  • App version, OS version, device model, crash logs, and high-level usage events. Used to debug crashes and improve the app.
  • We do not use third-party advertising trackers and we do not participate in Apple's IDFA tracking.

Information from third parties

  • OpenStreetMap / CartoDB: the map tiles your device fetches when viewing the map. They see your IP address.
  • Nominatim (OpenStreetMap): when you type a city or ZIP into the search box, that text + your IP is sent to OpenStreetMap's geocoder. We do not store the search term.

3. How We Use It

  • Run the app: show you tracks, deliver your follows' updates, render your profile, accept your posts.
  • Verify track ownership claims (manual review of documents you submit).
  • Fix bugs (crash logs).
  • Respond when you contact support.
  • Enforce our Community Guidelines (e.g., investigate reports of objectionable content).
  • Send you transactional notifications (e.g., a track owner you follow posts an update — only if you've enabled push).

We do not sell your data to anyone. Period.

4. Who Sees Your Content

  • Your profile is public by default — anyone using MotoTrax can see your handle, bikes, classes, and tracks you ride. Make it private in Settings → Privacy → Public Profile.
  • Posts, comments, reviews you submit on a track are public to all MotoTrax users.
  • Ride log / sessions default to "visible to your followers." Hide them entirely under Settings → Privacy → Ride Log Visible to Followers.
  • Precise location of where you check in is hidden by default; tracks show approximate location unless you flip Settings → Privacy → Show Precise Location.

5. Service Providers We Use

We rely on a small set of vendors to run the app. Each one only sees the data they need.

  • Firebase (Google) — Authentication, database, crash reports, basic analytics. Hosted in Google's US data centers. See Google Privacy Policy.
  • Apple Sign In — Authentication for "Sign in with Apple." See Apple Privacy Policy.
  • OpenStreetMap / CartoDB — Map tiles and geocoding when you search. Hosted by the OSM Foundation. See OSM Privacy Policy.

We do not share data with anyone else for marketing or advertising.

6. Your Rights & Controls

You can:

  • Edit any field on your profile from Settings → Account.
  • Toggle visibility of your profile, ride log, and precise location under Settings → Privacy.
  • Export everything we have on you — sessions, posts, follows, reviews — as a CSV file. Settings → Privacy & Data → Export My Data.
  • Delete your account in-app. Settings → Privacy & Data → Delete Account and confirm. Deletion removes your profile, posts, comments, reviews, sessions, and follows. Track ownership claims are revoked. We confirm deletion by email within 7 days.
  • Opt out of analytics via Settings → Privacy → Usage Analytics.

If you live in California (CCPA), the EU/UK (GDPR), or another jurisdiction with privacy rights, you have the same rights here regardless: access, deletion, correction, portability. Email info@nubric.dev if you'd rather make a request that way.

7. Retention

  • Active account data is kept while the account exists.
  • Deleted accounts are removed from our active database within 30 days of the deletion request. Backups are purged within 90 days.
  • Crash logs and analytics are kept for 14 months, then deleted.

8. Security

We use Firebase's encryption at rest and TLS in transit. Authentication tokens are stored in your device's secure storage (Keychain on iOS, Keystore on Android). No system is perfect — if we ever discover a breach affecting your data, we'll notify you within 72 hours of confirming the issue.

9. Children

MotoTrax is not directed at children under 13. If you are a parent and believe your child has created an account, email info@nubric.dev and we'll delete it.

10. Changes to This Policy

We'll update this page when we change anything material. The "Last updated" date at the top will change. If the change is significant, we'll also send an in-app notification.
